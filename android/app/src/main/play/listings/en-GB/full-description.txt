Income & expense ledger for institutions

Muthal is a minimal ledger for tracking income and expenses across institutions such as temples, churches, libraries, and small non-profits. Create a ledger for each institution you manage, record entries, and see running balances update in real time.

Institutions can be shared with other people. An owner invites members by sharing a 6-character join code or a link, and assigns each person a role: owners manage the institution and its members, admins record and categorise entries, and members can view the ledger without making changes.

Categories are configurable per institution, so each entry can be filed under income or expense categories that make sense for that organisation. A period export shows the opening balance, closing balance, and every entry between any two dates you choose.

Muthal is available on Android and the web, using the same account on both. Feedback is welcome -- join the tester group at groups.google.com/g/muthal-testers.
